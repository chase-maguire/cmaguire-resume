School Artworks

Already Dead - March 2024

This piece is a recreation of a screenshot from the 1952 movie 'Ikiru'. As a divergence from the original screen grab, my final piece emphasizes the darkness in his eyes and on his face, reflecting how he feels after his cancer diagnosis in the film.

Doomslayer Text Portrait - November 2023

This piece is a project, done in Adobe Illustrator, that asked me to make a portrait of someone out of text that relate to or describe that person. Mine is of the character Doomslayer from the Doom franchise.

David Carson Style Magazine - March 2024

This four page magazine, created in Adobe Illustrator, attempts to replicate the art style of graphic artist David Carson, showcasing his biography, some of his achievments, and some of his previous works.
